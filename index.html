Description of Service

SafeSpacePH is a free online legal assistance platform that provides consultation related to the Safe Spaces Act (RA 11313) in the Philippines. It connects users with volunteer legal professionals for pro bono services. The Service seeks to help victims of gender-based sexual harassment obtain legal awareness and support as quickly as possible.
